Introduction

Hi, I'm Alex Morgan — I turn ideas into real software.

I'm a third-year Computer Science student at State University with a deep passion for full-stack web development and software engineering. I started coding at 15 out of curiosity, and since then it's become both my biggest hobby and the career I'm building toward.

My experience spans frontend frameworks like React and Vue.js, backend development with Node.js and Python, and working with databases ranging from relational (PostgreSQL) to NoSQL (MongoDB). I love the process of turning a rough idea into a clean, working product that real people use.

Outside of coding I enjoy reading about AI research, contributing to open source, and collaborating with other developers. I'm currently looking for internship opportunities where I can grow as an engineer and make a meaningful contribution.
